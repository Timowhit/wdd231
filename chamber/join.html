Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the membership last?

All membership levels are annual subscriptions. Your membership begins on the date your application is approved and runs for 12 months. We'll send renewal reminders 30 days before expiration.

Can I upgrade my membership later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade your membership at any time. When you upgrade, you'll pay the prorated difference for the remainder of your membership term and immediately receive all the benefits of your new level.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and ACH bank transfers. For Gold members, we also offer quarterly payment plans at no additional cost.

Do I need to have a physical location in Gilbert?

While we primarily serve Gilbert-based businesses, we welcome businesses from the greater East Valley area that serve Gilbert residents. Home-based businesses are also welcome!

How do I get my business featured in the spotlight?

Silver and Gold members are automatically considered for our member spotlight features. Gold members receive guaranteed monthly spotlights, while Silver members are featured quarterly. Contact our team to schedule your feature!
